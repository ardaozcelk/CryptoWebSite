Arbitrage Trading

Arbitrage trading is a trading strategy that takes advantage of price differences in different markets. Example of triangle arbitrage: The price of Bitcoin on Exchange A is 5% higher than on Exchange B, so the investor buys Bitcoin at a lower price on Exchange B, transfers it to Exchange A and sells it. If a trader has funds on several exchanges, this arbitrage strategy can be automated.
